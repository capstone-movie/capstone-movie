Alex is new to anime and wants to find a show that matches his personal preferences. He visits the **AniRec** website/app, explores the *beginner-friendly recommendations*, and quickly browses through popular titles. After reviewing a few options, he decides to watch one that catches his interest.

Step 1: Alex clicks and opens up **AniRec**

System Response: The homepage loads, showing a welcoming message, a search bar, trending anime displayed from left to right at the top, and a section for beginners.

Step 2: Alex briefly scrolls through the homepage scanning through, then decides to click on the *Beginner-Friendly Recommendations* section

System Response: AniRec loads a brief trailer as well as loading user reviews and suggest similar anime below
